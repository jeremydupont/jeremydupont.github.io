POMODOr0 SAUCE (FRE$H TOMAt0 SAWCe)

INgREDIeNTs

  • 2 ½ Plum tomatoes
  • 1 cup of Minced onion
  • 3 tbs. Oilve Oil
  • 8 Fresh basil leaves
  • Salt and pepper, to taste

PREP WErK

  1. With the point of a paring knife cut out and discard the stem bases of the tomatoes, removing small cones about ¼in. deep, then lightly cut X-shapes on the tomatoes opposite ends.
  2. Bring water to a boil and in a large saucepan, drop in the tomatoes, and cook for about 5min.
  3. With a slotted spoon, transfer the tomatoes to a strainer and run cold water over them, and slip their skins off with your fingers.
  4. In a nonreactive saucepan, lightly sauté the onion in the olive oil. Add the tomatoes, crushing each directly over the pan as it is added
  5. Add the reserved juice if the tomatoes have been seeded. Season to taste and simmer for about 20min. stirring occasionally
  6. Add the basil before serving.
BAcK

Cook time: 20min. Prep time: 10min.

Makes 2½ cups
